# Strategy Pattern

## Definition

The Strategy Pattern defines a family of algorithms and makes them interchangeable.

The algorithm can be selected at runtime.

## Why Use Strategy?

Avoids large if-else chains.

Makes it easier to add new behaviors without modifying existing code.

## Advantages

* Flexible behavior selection
* Easy maintenance
* Follows Open Closed Principle

## Real World Example

Google Maps offers:

* Car Route
* Bike Route
* Walking Route

The route calculation strategy changes based on user selection.
